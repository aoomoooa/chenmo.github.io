PosterBot — election billboards

2025 · installation, automated image system · nuremberg

PosterBot is an automated system that continuously replaces faces and images on German election billboards with generated portraits. It runs on a local pipeline that watches for new captures, swaps foreground and background, and triggers ComfyUI workflows.

The work approaches political posters as a kind of infrastructure rather than mere images: something that quietly occupies public space, is overseen by regulations, and yet remains visually neglected by most passers-by.
